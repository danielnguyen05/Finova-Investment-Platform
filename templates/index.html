FINOVA Trading Project

FINOVA is a data-driven financial analysis platform that integrates real-time data from the Alpha Vantage API to visualize key financial trends through interactive graphs, and includes a fine-tuned AI chatbot for user inquiries related to finance. Additionally, the platform assesses user risk tolerance and recommends an investment portfolio by selecting assets such as companies and ETF's with the highest return rates in their category.

Platform Features

Real-Time Financial and Market Data Extraction


The platform continuously fetches financial and real estate data from various APIs, ensuring that users have up-to-date insights into stock performance, treasury yields, and portfolio return rates. This data is processed and structured to highlight key trends, enabling users to make informed investment decisions. With automatic updates, the platform eliminates the need for manual research, providing a seamless experience for investors.

AI-Powered Market Insights & Chatbot


Users can interact with a highly-trained AI chatbot that leverages Ollama and LangChain to provide real-time investment insights and answer queries about market trends, portfolio strategies, and asset performance. The chatbot retains context across conversations, ensuring personalized and intelligent responses. This feature allows both beginners and experienced investors to receive expert-like advice tailored to their investment goals.

Interactive Graphs and Visualizations


The platform transforms raw market data into intuitive visualizations, helping users track asset performance and compare investment opportunities. Customizable charts allow investors to analyze historical returns, risk-adjusted gains, and market trends in real-time. This feature enhances decision-making by providing clear, data-driven insights at a glance.

Personalized Portfolio Recommendation System


Based on the user's risk tolerance—conservative, moderate, or aggressive—the platform suggests an optimized investment portfolio. It analyzes historical returns, volatility, and market conditions to recommend stocks and ETF assets with the highest potential for returns. This ensures users can build a portfolio that aligns with their financial goals while managing risk effectively.
